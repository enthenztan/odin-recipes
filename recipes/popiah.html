Description

Popiah, also known as Fresh Spring Rolls, is a delightful dish featuring soft spring roll wrappers filled with a variety of fresh vegetables, tofu, and a flavorful hoisin sauce.

Ingredients

  • 8 spring roll wrappers
  • 200g jicama (yambean), julienned
  • 150g carrots, julienned
  • 100g bean sprouts
  • 100g tofu, sliced
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Crushed peanuts
  • Lettuce leaves

Steps

  1. Soften spring roll wrappers according to package instructions.
  2. Lay a wrapper flat, spread hoisin sauce, add lettuce, tofu, jicama, carrots, and bean sprouts.
  3. Fold sides and roll tightly. Repeat for remaining ingredients.
  4. Sprinkle crushed peanuts on top.
  5. Serve with extra hoisin sauce for dipping.
