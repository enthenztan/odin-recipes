Nasi Lemak Recipe

Description

Nasi Lemak is a beloved Malaysian dish known for its fragrant coconut rice served with an array of delicious condiments. This flavorful and satisfying dish is a staple in Malaysian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups jasmine rice, washed and drained
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 1 pandan leaf, knotted
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Hard-boiled eggs
  • Cucumber slices
  • Fried anchovies
  • Roasted peanuts
  • Sambal (spicy chili paste)

Steps

  1. In a rice cooker, combine rice, coconut milk, pandan leaf, water, and salt. Cook until done.
  2. Serve rice with hard-boiled eggs, cucumber slices, fried anchovies, and roasted peanuts.
  3. Spoon sambal over the rice or serve on the side.
